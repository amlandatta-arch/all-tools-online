Advanced Age Calculator

Calculate exact age in years, months and days. Compare dates, check next birthday and find your zodiac sign instantly.

What this calculator does

This tool gives an accurate age breakdown from any date of birth: years, months and days, plus total months, total days, weeks, hours and minutes. It also lets you compare any two dates (handy for contracts, eligibility checks or historical comparisons), tells you the weekday you were born on, and displays your western zodiac sign.

When to use it

Use it when you need precise age calculations for applications (school, job eligibility, visas), for event planning (counting days to an anniversary or birthday), or for curiosity ‚Äî for example, ‚ÄúHow many days old am I?‚Äù. Because calculations run in the browser, your data never leaves your device.

How accurate is it?

Accuracy follows the Gregorian calendar rules used by JavaScript Date. Months are calculated using calendar month lengths; total months uses a 30.4375 average month for a useful approximation when presenting totals.
