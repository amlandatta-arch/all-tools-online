About the Age Calculator

This Age Calculator lets you instantly find your exact age from your date of birth to any target date. It calculates years, months, days, hours, minutes, and even seconds between two dates. Whether you need to verify age for official purposes, track milestones, or just satisfy curiosity — this tool gives you accurate, instant results.

The calculator uses precise time functions to handle leap years, month length variations, and date offsets. You can select both the birth date and a target date manually or through the built-in calendar pickers. Everything runs locally in your browser, so none of your data is saved or shared.

It’s fully responsive and designed to work on any device — desktop, tablet, or smartphone. Ideal for calculating age for official forms, job applications, visa forms, or school registrations. With second-by-second precision, it’s one of the most detailed and privacy-safe online age calculators available.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this Age Calculator free?

Yes, it’s 100% free to use. There are no hidden fees, ads, or registration requirements.

Can I use this calculator offline?

You can save this page to your device and it will still work, as all calculations are done locally in your browser.

Does it account for leap years?

Yes. The algorithm accurately handles leap years and varying month lengths.

Is my data saved anywhere?

No. All input and results remain in your browser only — nothing is uploaded or stored.

Can I check age on a specific date?

Yes. You can choose any date to calculate your age as of that day — past or future.
